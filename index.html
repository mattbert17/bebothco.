Built to Outlast the Engagement

Capability, not dependency

We look at what is actually driving behaviour before we talk about changing it. Insight comes before action, always. Most organizations are solving the wrong problem.

We work inside your team, not from a distance. Our consultants embed with you — attending your meetings, observing your culture, and building trust that makes honest feedback possible.

Every senior consultant has led inside an organization. We've sat in the seats you're sitting in. That changes how we listen and what we notice.

The goal is never to create reliance on us. Every engagement is designed to build internal capability so your team can sustain the gains long after we've left.

Who We Are

Built by people
who've been
in the room.

Be Both exists because we spent years watching capable leaders struggle — not from lack of skill, but from lack of clarity. The issue was rarely what they were doing. It was the patterns underneath their decisions.

We have worked inside organizations across sectors: Fortune 500s, high-growth ventures, public institutions, and family-owned businesses. What interested us most was never the org chart — it was the people doing the work.

That interest led us to build a practice grounded in organizational behavior, leadership psychology, and the hard-won lessons of real operational experience. We write, consult, and create learning environments that help leaders see themselves and their organizations more clearly.

Our approach is practical, human, and relentlessly focused on building the awareness and capacity that leads to real, lasting change in how organizations lead and operate.
